ASTRUM-005 was an international phase 3, double-blind, randomized clinical trial spanning 114 hospital sites across 6 countries.1

The study objective was to evaluate the efficacy of the PD‑1 inhibitor serplulimab plus chemotherapy compared with placebo plus chemotherapy as first-line treatment in patients with extensive-stage small-cell lung cancer (ES-SCLC)1

Now, its findings are being leveraged in the ASTRIDE bridging trial to confirm the applicability of the results to ES-SCLC patients in the United States.2

DISCOVER ASTRUM-005 NOW

Serplulimab is an investigational therapy in the US and is not FDA approved. The ASTRUM-005 trial provides important research on its potential treatment for ES-SCLC.
